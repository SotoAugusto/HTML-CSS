stroke

the equivalent to border, these 3 are the same length but using different ends to the line (stroke-linecap)
first butt
second square, which leaves more 'margin' to the ends
third rounds the ends also leaving a extra 'margin'

control how the joins between two lines are drawn with stroke-linejoin
first miter, which creates a square corner
second round, creates a rounded corner
third bevel, creates a new angle to aid in the transition between the two segments.

fill

the equivalent to background, sets the color inside the object
