pseudo-class nth child

Provides options to to target, *even* child of tags, *odd* child, *first* or *last*, or some amount of *n* number of child.

li:first-child{ color:red;

li:last-child{ background-color: yellowgreen;

ul:first-child{ background-color: limegreen;
